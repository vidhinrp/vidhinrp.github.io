Below are some data analysis projects I've recently worked on.

Water Wars: Using data from the Pacific Insitute, I conducted analysis on armed conflicts associated with water resources and water systems, as identified from news reports, eyewitness accounts, and other conflict databases. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated: Pandas, Seaborn, GeoPandas, Text Mining.

311 Saves the Day: Using 311 complaint data from the Charlotte City website for 2018, I performed ESDA and developed automated processes for reusability for 311 requests in other years and/or cities. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated: Pandas, Seaborn, Numpy.

UNC Staff Salaries: Using publicly available data on salaries of employees at UNC-Chapel Hill, I conduncted a rudimentry ESDA.I sought to gauge relationships between salary and factors such as year employed and department employed. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated: Tidyverse.

FinCEN Files: Using publicly available data on a compilation of 2,500 documents that banks sent to U.S authorities between 2000-2017 highlighting suspicious client activities, I, along with three other team members, conducted analysis and created a rudimentary model. We explored whether it was possible to predict the beneficiary country by identifying the originator country as well as the number of transactions they made. We also wanted to highlight the relationship between the number of banks involved in a country, its GDP, its haven score, its global scale weight and the average or mean transacted amount. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated: Tidyverse.

Progress made on Sustainable Development Goal 7.3: In 2015, the United Nations set a series of 17 interconnected development goals for the world called the "Sustainable Development Goals" (SDG). Using data from the Global SDG Indicators Database, I created a multi-page dashboard on Tableau. The dashboard focuses on one specific target of the SDGs focused on improving energy efficiency. All data cleaning and transforming was done on Tableau as a means to test how far the tool can go. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated: Tableau.
