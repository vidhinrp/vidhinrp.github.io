Below are some projects I've recently worked on.

Water Wars: Using data from the Pacific Insitute, I conducted analysis on armed conflicts associated with water resources and water systems,as identified from news reports, eyewitness accounts, and other conflict databases. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated Pandas, Seaborn, GeoPandas, Text Mining.
311 Saves the Day: Using 311 complaint data from the Charlotte City website for 2018, I performed ESDA and developed automated processes for reusability for 311 requests in other years and/or cities. Libraries/Skills Demonstrated Pandas, Seaborn, Numpy.
